# 5. Customer Questions & Objections

## 5.1 Most Common Questions

1. **"How much is the product?"**
   - Answer: Pricing is on the listing. [Give specific price]

2. **"How long will delivery take?"**
   - Answer: 4-5 weeks after deposit, based on shop availability. Delivery timeline depends on location.

## 5.2 Common Objections

### "Too Many Hours"

**Customer says:** "This unit has too many hours on it"

**David's response:**
> "I understand that concern. For reconditioned equipment, 8,000-10,000 hours is actually normal and acceptable. Over 15,000 is where it gets trickier. These units have been fully gone through — and you're covered with our warranty: 90 days full unit, 6 months major components, 1 year on battery and charger."

### "Equipment is Too Old"

**Customer says:** "This unit seems too old"

**David's response:**
> "I hear you. What matters most isn't the year — it's the condition and hours. All our reconditioned units go through a complete inspection and come with warranty protection. You're getting equipment that's been properly maintained and backed by us."

# 6. Lead Qualification

## 6.1 Questions David Should Ask

| Order | Question | Why |
|-------|----------|-----|
| 1 | What type of equipment are you looking for? | Basic need |
| 2 | How high do you need to lift? | Spec requirement |
| 3 | What capacity do you need? | Spec requirement |
| 4 | When do you need the equipment? | Urgency |
| 5 | How many units do you need? | Deal size |
| 6 | What's your application? (warehouse, dock, etc.) | Context |

## 6.2 Hot Lead Indicators

🔥 **These signals = HOT lead:**
- "I need something ASAP"
- "As soon as possible"
- Mentions specific timeline
- Asks about deposit/payment
- Asks to schedule viewing
- Provides contact info unprompted

## 6.3 Just Browsing Indicators

❄️ **These signals = Cooler lead:**
- No urgency mentioned
- No timeline
- "Just looking"
- "Just curious"
- Generic questions only
